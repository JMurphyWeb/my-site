    15 August 2015

    Website is up and ready, far from perfect but I will look to iron out any problems in the coming weeks. I think I will post the site on Reddit’s r/learn-web-dev forum which I hope will provide some useful feedback.

It’s probably been lurking somewhere at the bottom of several to-do lists for a few months now, along with making granola bars.
